About Us

Milagrosa Happiness Private Resort

The Milagrosa Happiness Private Resort is a nature-friendly and beautiful resort located in Talisay Street, Peñabatan Pulilan, Bulacan. Romeo "OME" Santos, the owner, started the operation of the Milagrosa Happiness Private Resort on April 20, 2023. This resort is suitable for vacations, birthdays, Christmas parties, and other events. Milagrosa Happiness Private Resort was built to start a business and to provide a beautiful view and unforgettable joyful experience to the guests who will visit here.

Our Mission

To delight every guest to be relaxed and happy during their stay, offering friendly service with unforgettable experiences that create lasting memories of joy and comfort.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the premier destination and most accommodating private resort in Pulilan, Bulacan, known for exceptional service, beautiful surroundings, and unforgettable guest experiences.

Our Core Values

Hospitality

We treat every guest like family, providing warm, personalized service that exceeds expectations.

Sustainability

We maintain an eco-friendly resort that harmonizes with nature while providing modern comforts.

Joy

We create an atmosphere of happiness where guests can relax, celebrate, and make memories.

Excellence

We continuously improve our facilities and services to maintain the highest standards.
